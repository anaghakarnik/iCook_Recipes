classic penne alfredo pasta

A rich and creamy Italian delicacy that is easy and healthy to make for a perfect lunch or dinner.

Recipe Description

Penne pasta covered in creamy alfredo sauce made from sauteed green bell peppers over butter and garlic, fresh milk/cream, parmesan and mozzarella cheeses, and dashed with crushed red peppers, black pepper, and parsley.

prep time

15 min.
cook time

30 min.
servings

Serves 6
ingredients
  • 454 g / 1 lb penne pasta
  • 6 cups water
  • 2-3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup square sliced bell peppers
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tsp parsley
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt to taste
instructions

step 1

Take water in a big container and place over the stove on high heat. Bring the water to boil and then add about 1 tbsp of salt and vegetable oil in the water. Add pasta and cook for about 15-20 minutes till the pasta is cooked just enough, 'al dente'. Drain the pasta with cold water once cooked.

step 2

Melt butter on pan over a low heat. Then sautee minced garlic, spices, and bell peppers in the butter.

step 3

Add flour and milk in the pan. Bring to a boil. Mix in the cheeses and add salt to taste.

step 4

Once the alfredo sauce is ready, fold in the cooked penne pasta.

step 5

While serving, garnish with some more cheese and spices, then enjoy!
